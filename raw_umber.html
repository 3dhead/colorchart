CHEMICAL FORMULA/DESCRIPTION

Fe2O3.xMnO2 Ferroso ferric oxide prepared from ores containing up to 25 percent manganese dioxide and varying amounts of alumina and silica

OIL FILM CHARACTERISTICS

Fast drying Hard. fairly flexible

VEHICLE COMPATIBILITY

Linseed oil, alkyd. acrylic, watercolor, gouache. tempera. casein. encaustic. fresco, pastel

TOXICITY

Manganese is considered toxic: do not breathe dust

COMMON NAMES

Raw umber, natural brown iron oxide Raw umber. A native earth. its composition is similar to sienna but it contains manganese. A dark brown its tones vary from greenish or yellowish to violet-brown. Not entirely opaque. Good grades come from Italy. The best grade (Turkey umber) comes from Cyprus.
